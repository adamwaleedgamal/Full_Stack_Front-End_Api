Home Delivery

Our Home Delivery service allows an employee to visit your home, collect recyclable materials, and provide payment for them. This convenient option ensures you can recycle without leaving your house.

Rewards

Our Rewards program recognizes and rewards the best recycling centers in our cluster. By encouraging healthy competition, we motivate recycling centers to strive for excellence and contribute significantly to environmental sustainability.

Awareness

Our Awareness initiative focuses on educating the public about recycling simple materials at home. We provide valuable resources and information to help everyone understand the importance of recycling and take action in their own households.
